Dedicated to preserving traditional Asian fermentation techniques while adapting them for modern Singapore living. Join our community of fermentation enthusiasts.

Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2020, Home Fermentation Lab Singapore was born from a passion for preserving traditional Asian fermentation techniques in our modern tropical environment. Our founders recognized that ancient wisdom needed contemporary adaptation to thrive in Singapore's unique climate.

We believe that fermentation is more than just food preparation‚Äîit's a connection to cultural heritage, a path to better health, and a sustainable way of living. Our mission is to make these time-honored techniques accessible to every Singaporean household.

Through our workshops, we've trained over 500 students in authentic Korean kimchi making, Indonesian tempeh cultivation, and Japanese miso production, all carefully adapted for Singapore's tropical conditions.

Meet Our Expert Team

Our certified instructors bring years of experience and authentic training from across Asia

Sarah Kim

Lead Kimchi Instructor

Trained in traditional kimchi making in Busan, Korea. Sarah has adapted authentic Korean techniques for Singapore's tropical climate, specializing in vegetable selection and fermentation timing.

  • Traditional Korean kimchi techniques
  • Tropical climate adaptations
  • Vegetable fermentation timing

Ahmad Rahman

Tempeh Master

Born in Java, Indonesia, Ahmad brings authentic tempeh-making knowledge to Singapore. His expertise in spore cultivation and humidity control makes him Singapore's premier tempeh instructor.

  • Traditional Indonesian tempeh methods
  • Spore cultivation techniques
  • Humidity and temperature control

Yuki Tanaka

Miso & Koji Specialist

Certified in traditional Japanese fermentation from Kyoto, Yuki specializes in koji cultivation and long-term miso aging. Her knowledge of Japanese fermentation science is unparalleled in Southeast Asia.

  • Traditional Japanese miso production
  • Koji cultivation and maintenance
  • Long-term aging techniques
